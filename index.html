About Me!

My childhood

My name is Richard Villagomez, and I was born and raised on the island of Saipan in the CNMI, or the Commonwealth of the Northern Marianas Islands. I lived there for 17 years, which I still can't believe. Saipan is a 12-mile long island in the middle of the Pacific Ocean, somewhat near South Asia/Oceania. Fun fact: The Marianas Trench is named after the Marianas Islands!

My hobbies

Some of my favorite things to do during my free time include playing my guitar, going on runs, watching thrillers or chick-flicks on Netflix, or playing on my Switch.
